Accredited Trainings for ISO Standards

This training page provides information about various accredited trainings for different ISO standards.

ISO 9001 - Quality Management System

ISO 9001 is an international standard that specifies requirements for a quality management system (QMS). It helps organizations demonstrate their commitment to quality and continuous improvement.

ISO 28000 - Supply Chain Security Management

ISO 28000 provides a framework for managing security in the supply chain. It helps organizations address security risks and ensure the safety of goods and services throughout the supply chain.

ISO 14001 - Environmental Management System

ISO 14001 sets out criteria for an environmental management system. It helps organizations manage their environmental responsibilities and contribute to sustainable development.

ISO/IEC 20000 - IT Service Management

ISO/IEC 20000 provides a framework for managing IT service management processes. It helps organizations deliver efficient and high-quality IT services to their clients and customers.

ISO/IEC 27001 - Information Security Management System

ISO/IEC 27001 outlines requirements for establishing, implementing, maintaining, and continuously improving an information security management system. It helps organizations manage and protect their sensitive information.

ISO/IEC 27005 - Risk Management in Information Security

ISO/IEC 27005 provides guidelines for information security risk management. It assists organizations in identifying and managing risks related to information security.

ISO 22301 - Business Continuity Management System

ISO 22301 specifies requirements for a business continuity management system. It helps organizations prepare for and respond to disruptions, ensuring the continued delivery of products and services.

ISO 26000 - Social Responsibility

ISO 26000 provides guidance on social responsibility and sustainable development. It helps organizations understand and address their social and environmental impacts.

ISO 22000 - Food Safety Management System

ISO 22000 outlines requirements for a food safety management system. It helps organizations ensure the safety of food products throughout the supply chain.

ISO 45001 - Occupational Health and Safety Management System

ISO 45001 sets out requirements for an occupational health and safety management system. It helps organizations provide safe and healthy workplaces for their employees.
